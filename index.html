More About Us

A Holistic Haven for Health, Fitness & Well-being

Established in 2022 after years of vision and research, Avya Club is Nepal’s pioneering recreational and wellness centre. Spanning over 110,000 sq.ft with a panoramic view of the Himalayas, we offer a transformative experience focused on fitness, mindfulness, and lifestyle — all under one roof.

To create a holistic space where individuals and families can experience physical transformation, mental clarity, and emotional balance through advanced facilities, compassionate support, and community engagement.

To be Nepal’s most trusted well-being destination that transcends traditional gym culture — blending fitness, nutrition, mental wellness, and family inclusiveness to promote a lifestyle of total health and happiness.

Our goal is to serve as a sanctuary where every individual, from first-time gym-goers to elite athletes, finds a supportive environment to thrive, grow, and rejuvenate — mind, body, and soul.
